About Gardens Gymnastics

Gardens Gymnastics is a children’s gym specializing in Parent/ Child, Preschool and Recreational classes. Our philosophy is to teach gymnastics and children’s fitness in a CLEAN, SAFE learning environment . Our staff will always try to challenge your children at their individual level and help build their self confidence . We pride ourselves on low student/ teacher ratio and lots of individualized attention. In addition to gymnastics and fitness, your child will build Strength, flexibility, coordination, while learning about teamwork and sportsmanship.

About the Instructors

Ms. Patti

Owner/ Director

Ms. Patti Wallshein has been teaching gymnastics and children’s fitness for over 36 years. She graduated from Tulane University with a degree in communications and marketing. She has two beautiful girls Jen and Katie, a loving husband Bill and Calli her wheaten terrier. Her greatest pride is teaching a second generation of children in the Palm Beach County area. She uses a safe and encouraging method to teach gymnastics and fitness in a positive atmosphere. Her classes include strength training, flexibility and skill progressions, all of which build a healthy mind and body.

Ms. Tara

Head Coach

Miss Tara Calafiore was a competitive gymnast for 3 years and has been coaching gymnastics for 30 years. She graduated college with a degree in Health and Fitness. She teaches all levels from Parent/ Child classes to advanced classes. Her philosophy is to teach the correct foundation for all skills and progress in a positive, safe learning environment . She has two fur babies Oliver and Beau who she loves very much.
